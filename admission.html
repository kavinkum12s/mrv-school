Admission

Our School Admission


 Admissions in MRV VELLALAR MATRICULATION HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL are done without any distinction of religion, cast, or creed. Normally admissions are done only in LKG. The child should be 3+ at the time of admission. Registrations are done during the month of January, the details of which would be displayed on the school notice boards. Original birth certificates should be submitted at the time of registration. Students for whom admission is sought should be accompanied by the parents, who alone can sign the prescribed admission form and pledge to abide by the school rules. However, admissions to other classes are done to fill up the vacant seats only.

 Application forms for admission to the schools can be had from the School Office. Completed application forms should be sent to the School Office within a stipulated date. In case an application is rejected for admission in a particular year, a fresh application will have to be made for admission in the following year.

 The Board of Governors reserves the right to admit any child at any time. They also have the right to expel any child on the recommendation of the Principal.

 English is the medium of instruction. Along with English, Tamil and Hindi can be taken either as second or third language.

 Appropriate rate of fee will be fixed by the Board of Governors. Rate of fee once fixed will not be revised without giving 2 months prior notice in writing to the parents. All parents will be advised of the current rate of fee at the commencement of each academic year. 'T' shirts, Text Books & Note Books will have to be paid for and supplied by the school.



Fee Structure


 Fees charged for various classes is proportionate to the facilities provided. No donation or capitation fees charged by the school.

About school

Founded in 2009, MRV Higher SEC School is one of the premier schools in Perambalur, Tamil Nadu. Affiliated to the state board and owned by the private unaided body, this co-ed school makes the entire learning zone enthusiastic for the students from class 6th to 12th.
