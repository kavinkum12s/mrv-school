About us

Founded in 2009, MRV Higher Secondary School is one of the premier schools in Perambalur, Tamil Nadu.

Affiliated to the state board and owned by the private unaided body, this co-ed school makes the entire learning zone enthusiastic for the students from class 6th to 12th.

The school consists of a private and secured building with premium infrastructure and an excellent faculty team.
