Infrastructure


The uniqueness of the school's environmentally sound infrastructure lies in its design.Classrooms are spaciously built with good ventilation, natural light and with some of the most comfortable furntiure.All the classrooms are further equipped with modern teaching aids and equipments. The large premises of the school provides ample space for the administrative block, sufficient classrooms , a large playground for various sports activities, space for yoga along with the colourful and safe kids play area. The kindergarten section displays vibrant classrooms for young ones to make them understand the life of colours. So join us to master the skill of education.

Transport


MRV Matric Higher Secondary School provides safe and reliable bus transportation to students who wish to avail this facility. The school has buses with the best of safety arrangements available for the neighbouring areas. The students in school buses are accompanied by well-trained attendants/ escorts to ensure their safety.

Hostel


MRV Matriculation Higher Secondary School offers excellent hostel facilities for outstation students. Boys and girls are admitted as boarders in seperate hostels within the school campus. A student pursuing his/her studies at MRV Matriculation Higher Secondary School resides in the hostel owned and maintained by the school Management, during the studentship. Students staying in the hostel are comfortable in a conducive environment for a healthy living and discipline is maintained amongst the inmates. It^s a home away from home provided with good and nutritious food. It gives the student a pleasant atmosphere to study well and aim high.

Library


MRV Matriculation Higher Secondary School has a well-stocked library with interesting and informative books on a variety of subjects. Students are encouraged to refer to these books not only for academic purposes but also to discover the pleasure of reading in a serene atmosphere.
About school

Founded in 2009, MRV Higher SEC School is one of the premier schools in Perambalur, Tamil Nadu. Affiliated to the state board and owned by the private unaided body, this co-ed school makes the entire learning zone enthusiastic for the students from class 6th to 12th.
